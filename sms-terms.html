SMS / WhatsApp Terms & Conditions

Effective Date: August 23, 2025

1) Program Description

The Good Life Path (TGL) provides informational SMS/WhatsApp messages to deliver community health screening results categorized as Low/Medium/High. This program is strictly non-promotional.

2) Opt-In Methods

  • SMS Keyword: Text TGL to +1-551-383-2204. You will receive a confirmation request; reply YES to complete enrollment (double opt-in).
  • Paper Consent Form: Provide your mobile number and check the consent box acknowledging the disclosure printed on the form.

Consent is not a condition of purchase. Opt-in records are logged for compliance.

3) Message Frequency & Fees

Frequency: one opt-in confirmation, one result per screening, and responses to STOP/HELP. Message & data rates may apply.

4) Opt-Out

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP, END, CANCEL, QUIT, or UNSUBSCRIBE. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages unless you opt back in.

5) Help

Reply HELP or SUPPORT for assistance. You may also contact us at info@thegoodlifepath.org or call 510.255.0232.

6) Consumer Privacy

Required Disclosure:

Information sharing to subcontractors in support services (e.g., messaging providers, customer support) is permitted. All categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

7) Carrier Liability

Wireless carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Delivery depends on carrier/network conditions and device compatibility.

8) Eligibility

You must be the account holder or have the account holder’s permission to enroll the mobile number you provide. Program availability is limited to the United States.

9) Program Changes & Termination

We may modify or discontinue the messaging program at any time. Your right to opt out at any time remains unaffected.
