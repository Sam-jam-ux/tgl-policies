SMS / WhatsApp Terms & Conditions

Program Description

The Good Life Path (TGL) sends informational, non-promotional text messages containing your health screening results (categorized as Low/Medium/High) after explicit opt-in.

Opt-in

To enroll, text TGL to +1-XXX-XXX-XXXX and reply YES to confirm. Alternatively, provide your phone number on our paper consent form and check the consent box.

Frequency & Fees

Frequency: one result per screening plus opt-in confirmation and replies to STOP/HELP. Msg & Data rates may apply.

Opt-out

Text STOP, END, CANCEL, QUIT, or UNSUBSCRIBE at any time to stop receiving messages. You will receive a confirmation of your opt-out.

Help

Reply HELP or SUPPORT for assistance. You may also contact us at info@thegoodlifepath.org or +1-XXX-XXX-XXXX.

Privacy

No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. Information sharing to subcontractors in support services is permitted. All categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this will not be shared with any third parties.
