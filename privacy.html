Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 23, 2025

1) Introduction

The Good Life Path (“TGL”, “we”, “us”) operates a community program that delivers informational SMS/WhatsApp messages containing a participant’s health screening results. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, how we use and share it, and the choices available to you.

2) Information We Collect

  • Contact Information: Name, mobile number, and optional email provided during opt-in.
  • Consent & Messaging Records: Opt-in/opt-out events (e.g., keyword, YES confirmation, timestamps), HELP requests, delivery status, and related metadata required for compliance.
  • Program Data: Non-diagnostic screening result category (Low/Medium/High) and a summary link or image, if provided.
  • Technical Data: Carrier, device type, and system logs used to secure and operate the service.

3) How We Use Information

  • To deliver your health screening result and provide HELP/STOP responses.
  • To maintain consent logs and meet carrier/industry compliance requirements.
  • To operate, troubleshoot, and improve messaging reliability and security.
  • To comply with applicable laws and to protect the rights, safety, and integrity of our services.
A2P/CTIA Required Disclosure:
No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. Information sharing to subcontractors in support services (e.g., messaging providers, customer support) is permitted. All categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

4) Sharing of Information

  • Service Providers: We use vendors to send messages, host data, and provide support. They are bound by contract to use data only to provide services to us.
  • Legal/Compliance: We may disclose information as required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.
  • We do not sell personal information.

5) Your Choices & Rights

  • Opt-Out: Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time. You will receive a confirmation.
  • Help: Reply HELP for assistance.
  • Access/Deletion: You may request a copy of, or deletion of, your information subject to legal obligations and retention requirements.

6) Data Retention

We retain consent records and messaging logs for as long as needed to meet legal, regulatory, and carrier requirements, then delete or de-identify them.

7) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information. No system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

8) Children’s Privacy

Our program is intended for adults. If you believe a minor provided personal information without appropriate consent, contact us and we will remove it.

9) U.S. State Privacy Rights

Depending on your state, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information. To exercise rights, contact us using the information below.
