Privacy Policy

The Good Life Path (TGL) provides community health screening services and related informational messaging. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information: name, phone number, email (if provided).
  • Consent logs: opt-in confirmations, timestamps.
  • Screening summary: a categorized result (Low/Medium/High). No medical diagnosis.

Our SMS/WhatsApp Privacy Commitment

No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. Sharing to subcontractors in support services (e.g. customer service) is permitted. All categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this will not be shared with any third parties.
